What changed from v1 (answers to your 4 pain points)

  • 1   Less overwhelming — 3 steps stay, but Step 1 is split into 2 self-contained CARDS (Address + Schedule) with breathing room. Each card has its own micro-CTA so progress feels incremental.
  • 2   Date + time — custom horizontal day-picker (14 days, "Today/Tomorrow" labels) replaces native input. Time-windows are large cards with icons, real hours, and "X slots left" social proof.
  • 3   Trust + value — persistent badge bar across every step ("Free · 71-pt · 24h offer"). Reassurance line under each card. Brand strip at the bottom.
  • 4   Address autocomplete — Google-style typeahead with debounced suggestions, "Use my current location" button, static map preview to confirm. Free-text address fallback still available.
  • +   Sticky bottom CTA on mobile — Continue / Back always reachable without scrolling.
  • +   Tracker page — timeline visualisation, big bookingRef as anchor, WhatsApp deep-link, inspector card with photo + rating, status-aware CTAs.
