About this car

This 2022 Toyota Camry LE represents the ideal blend of reliability and comfort for Kuwaiti roads. With only 38,420 km on the clock and a single previous owner who maintained full Toyota dealer service history, this GCC-specification sedan has been looked after impeccably. The 2.5-litre four-cylinder engine delivers 203 hp with the effortless refinement Toyota is famous for, paired to a smooth-shifting automatic transmission that makes city commuting and highway cruising equally pleasurable.

The striking Midnight Black exterior is complemented by a premium Ivory interior, creating a cabin that feels both elegant and spacious. Standard LE equipment includes dual-zone automatic climate control, an 8-inch Toyota infotainment system with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, a pre-collision warning system, lane departure alert, and automatic high beams — features that place safety and connectivity front and centre.
